About Us

We Create Beautiful Spaces

Yoakin Interior Design & Finishing Works is a visionary professional interior design, renovation and construction company based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia. Founded in 2010 by Mr Bereket Engida Mersha with a commitment to elevate the standard of living and workspaces, Yoakin specializes in crafting modern, functional, and aesthetically pleasing environments that reflect style, purpose, and innovation. Starting from gypsum works to complete interior design, our team delivers quality and creativity for every project.

From residential interiors to commercial spaces, we provide a full suite of services—ranging from 3D design visualization and material selection to precision finishing and project execution. Our team of skilled designers, architects, and craftsmen collaborate closely with clients to bring their ideas to life while staying on schedule and within budget.

Driven by creativity, professionalism, and integrity, Yoakin transforms ordinary spaces into inspiring experiences. Every detail matters—because we believe the space you live or work in should be as remarkable as your vision.

Vision: To lead Ethiopia in modern, elegant, and culturally inspired interior design.

Mission: Deliver creative, high-quality, and sustainable finishing works that transform everyday spaces into beautiful experiences.
